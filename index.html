About me

I am a professional photographer specializing in portrait, wedding, and product photography. I have always had a passion for art and photography, and especially for the creativity and self-expression associated with artistic creation and experiences. This passion has deeply shaped my interest in pursuing photography more professionally and as a future career.

Learning about photography has provided me an outlet to learn something new and to constantly engage in a dialogue with my environment.
I graduated in professional photography from Dawson College in Montreal and have two years of professional photography experience.
